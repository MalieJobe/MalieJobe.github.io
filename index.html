About Me

My name is Malie Bertram, I am a software- & audio engineer. I started out at thirteen years old, building static websites in my free time. Later I got into CMS's which eventually brought me to real programming languages. ( Click to expand )

At the moment I am based in Berlin, Germany. I have a passion for music, technology and (human) languages, three of which I speak fluendly: German, English & Spanish (kind of). I got my degree in audio engineering out of pure interest and dove into the world of code right after. Since then I have been both self-employed & employed at mid-sized firms for multiple years.
